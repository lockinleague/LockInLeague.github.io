01

Who We Are

This Privacy Policy describes how Lock In League ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, stores, and protects personal information in connection with the Lock In League mobile application, related websites, legal pages, and connected services (together, the "Service").

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, you can contact us at lockinleague@proton.me.

02

Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to information we collect when you create or use an account, complete sessions, interact with rankings or social features, enable notifications, contact us, or otherwise use the Service.

It also applies to related public-facing legal or support pages operated by Lock In League where this Privacy Policy is linked or referenced.

03

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Email address
  • Username, handle, or display name
  • Profile details and customization choices, such as avatar selections
  • Account-related information you provide when signing up, signing in, or updating your profile
  • Information you submit through support requests, feedback, or communications with us
  • Session-related content, challenge details, or invite details you choose to enter into the Service

Information generated through your use of the Service

  • Session records, including session duration, timestamps, mode, completion status, interruptions, and performance results
  • Progress data such as points, streaks, leagues, promotions, demotions, rank movement, and leaderboard position
  • Social and competitive interaction data, including friend connections, challenge participation, invite use, and related in-app activity
  • Notification preferences and reminder settings

Technical and device information

  • Device type, operating system, app version, and similar technical information needed to operate and secure the Service
  • Push notification token or comparable messaging identifier if you enable notifications
  • Basic integrity, security, and troubleshooting information reasonably necessary to maintain the Service

Information from third parties

If you sign in using a supported third-party identity provider, we may receive basic account information made available by that provider, such as your email address, private relay address, or account identifier, subject to that provider's settings and permissions.

Local device storage

We may store limited information on your device, such as authentication state, notification preferences, and non-sensitive app settings, to support normal app functionality and improve continuity across sessions.

04

How We Use Information

We use personal information to:

  • Create, authenticate, and manage user accounts
  • Provide the core functionality of Lock In League
  • Maintain sessions, streaks, points, ranks, leagues, and leaderboard features
  • Support competitive and social functionality, including challenges and invitations where available
  • Send reminders, rank-related updates, or service notifications if you grant notification permission
  • Respond to support requests and user communications
  • Protect the security, integrity, reliability, and performance of the Service
  • Detect abuse, fraud, misuse, unauthorized access, or violations of our rules
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms, rules, and operational safeguards
We only use information in ways that are reasonably related to operating, improving, securing, and supporting the app experience.
05

How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information with third parties for their own advertising purposes.

Service providers

We may use third-party service providers to help operate the Service, including Google Firebase and related Google Cloud services for backend infrastructure, authentication, database functionality, cloud messaging, storage, hosting, and service operations.

Platform providers

We may share information with Apple or other platform providers where necessary for app functionality, app distribution, notifications, account security, sign-in, or legal compliance.

User-directed sharing

If you choose to share content such as a session result, invite, challenge, or share card, the information you choose to share may be made available through your selected device features or third-party apps.

Legal and safety reasons

We may disclose information if required to do so by law, regulation, legal process, or valid governmental request, or if reasonably necessary to protect the rights, safety, security, users, or integrity of the Service.

Business transfer

We may transfer information in connection with a merger, acquisition, restructuring, financing, sale of assets, or similar transaction, subject to applicable law.

06

Competitive and Social Visibility

Lock In League includes competitive and social elements. Depending on the features you use, some information may be visible to other users within the app.

This may include:

  • Your handle or display name
  • Your avatar or profile styling
  • Your rank, league, streak, or leaderboard position
  • Limited challenge, invite, or competitive activity information relevant to in-app features

We limit visibility to what is reasonably necessary to provide those features.

07

Notifications

If you enable notifications, we may use notification-related identifiers and your permission status to send reminders, rank-related updates, and service notifications.

You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

08

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Preserve account functionality and competitive progress
  • Respond to support requests and maintain reasonable support records
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce agreements and protect against abuse, fraud, or misuse

If you request deletion of your account or personal data, we will take reasonable steps to delete or anonymize your information, except where retention is required or permitted by law.
