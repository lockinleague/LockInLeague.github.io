Privacy Policy for Lock In League

Last updated: March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Lock In League ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, stores, and protects information in connection with the Lock In League mobile application and related services (the "Service").

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, you can contact us at lockinleague@proton.me.

1. Who We Are

Lock In League is operated by Lock In League.

Contact email: lockinleague@proton.me

2. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to information we collect when you use the Service, create or use an account, complete focus sessions, interact with ranked or social features, enable notifications, contact us, or otherwise use Lock In League.

3. Information We Collect

A. Information you provide directly

  • Email address
  • Username, handle, or display name
  • Profile details and customization choices, such as avatar selections
  • Information you provide when creating an account, signing in, contacting support, or using competitive or social features
  • Content you choose to enter into the Service, including session-related inputs, invite or challenge details, or support communications

B. Information generated through use of the Service

  • Session records, including duration, timestamps, mode, completion status, interruptions, and performance results
  • Progress and ranking data, including points, streaks, leagues, promotions, demotions, and leaderboard position
  • Social and competitive interaction data, including friend connections, invites, challenge participation, and related in-app activity
  • Notification preferences and reminder settings

C. Technical and device information

  • Device type, operating system, app version, and general technical information needed to operate and secure the Service
  • Push notification token or similar device messaging identifier if you enable notifications
  • Basic service integrity, security, and troubleshooting information

D. Information from third parties

If you sign in through a supported third-party identity provider, we may receive basic account information made available by that provider, such as your email address or account identifier, subject to your provider settings and permissions.

4. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Create, authenticate, and manage accounts
  • Provide the core features of Lock In League
  • Maintain sessions, points, streaks, rankings, leagues, and leaderboard features
  • Support competitive and social functionality
  • Send reminders or service notifications if you allow them
  • Respond to support requests and user communications
  • Protect the security, integrity, and reliability of the Service
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, unauthorized access, or misuse
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms and policies

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information with third parties for their own advertising purposes.

We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

A. Service providers

We may use third-party service providers to help operate the Service, including Google Firebase and related Google Cloud services for backend infrastructure, authentication, database functionality, notifications, storage, and service operations.

B. Platform providers

We may share information with Apple or other platform providers where necessary for app functionality, app distribution, notifications, account security, sign-in, or legal compliance.

C. User-directed sharing

If you choose to share content such as a session result, rank result, invite, challenge, or share card, the information you choose to share may be made available through your selected device feature or third-party sharing destination.

D. Legal or safety reasons

We may disclose information if required to do so by law, regulation, legal process, or valid governmental request, or if reasonably necessary to protect the rights, property, safety, users, or integrity of the Service.

E. Business transfer

We may transfer information in connection with a merger, acquisition, restructuring, financing, sale of assets, or similar transaction, subject to applicable law.

6. Competitive and Social Visibility

Lock In League includes competitive and social elements. Depending on the features you use, certain information may be visible to other users within the app.

This may include:

  • Your handle or display name
  • Your avatar or profile styling
  • Your rank, league, streak, or leaderboard position
  • Limited challenge, invite, or competitive activity information relevant to in-app features

We only make information visible where needed to provide those features.

7. Notifications

If you enable notifications, we may use notification-related identifiers and your permission status to send reminders, progress-related alerts, and service notifications. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

8. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Preserve account functionality and competitive progress
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce agreements and protect against abuse or fraud

If you request deletion of your account or personal data, we will take reasonable steps to delete or anonymize your information, except where retention is required or permitted by law.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information. These measures may include secure cloud infrastructure, authenticated access controls, encryption in transit, and backend authorization controls.

However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own, including countries where our service providers operate. By using the Service, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in those locations, subject to applicable legal safeguards where required.

11. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information.

You may also control certain permissions directly through your device settings, including notification permissions.

To submit a privacy-related request, contact us at lockinleague@proton.me.

12. Children's Privacy

Lock In League is not directed to children under 13, or under the minimum age required by applicable law in the user's jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below the applicable minimum age.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information to us, contact us at lockinleague@proton.me, and we will investigate and take appropriate action.
